# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Threadmill to be a practical, respectful project for everyone who uses,
reports issues, reviews code, or contributes changes.

Participants are expected to communicate with care, assume good intent where
reasonable, and keep technical disagreements focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Welcome clear bug reports and careful disagreement.
- Keep discussions relevant to Threadmill and its users.
- Accept maintainer decisions once a direction has been chosen.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests;
or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner.
